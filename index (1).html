Stalker at Home

A Psychological Horror, Adventure Game

Experience the terror of being stalked in your own home. As Emma, you must discover the truth behind the strange occurrences in your apartment while avoiding your abusive ex, Ethan.

Key Features

Explore a Haunting Apartment

Interact with a fully realized 3D environment and discover clues as you piece together the truth.

Uncover a Deeply Personal Story

Experience a narrative that explores themes of abuse, gaslighting, and trauma in a sensitive and respectful way.

Outsmart a Dangerous Foe

Utilize stealth, puzzle-solving, and quick thinking to outmaneuver Ethan and avoid his violent outbursts.

Experience a Compelling Narrative

Immerse yourself in a story that blurs the line between reality and delusion, with multiple endings based on your choices.
